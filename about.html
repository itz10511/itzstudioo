About

Itzel
Villanueva

Photographer · Videographer

Hey, i'm Itzel — a Hispanic photographer and videographer based in Southern California. i grew up surrounded by the rave and creative scenes of Los Angeles, and that culture shaped the way i see people, energy, and storytelling. Music, movement, and community have always been a huge part of how i capture and tell stories — it taught me that the best visuals don't just look good, they feel real and create fomo.

For me, great work starts with understanding the person behind the project. Your brand, your audience, your vision — all of it matters. i like getting to know the people i work with so the final result actually reflects who you are, not just what the camera sees.

Whether it's a festival, a brand campaign, a live show, or a personal project — my goal is always the same: capture the energy, the atmosphere, and the feeling of being there. From 35mm film to digital edits, i'm here to make sure the moments that matter are captured in a way that lasts.
